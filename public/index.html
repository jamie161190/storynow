Common questions

Can I hear it before I pay?+

Yes. You get a free preview of the opening of your story before you spend anything. Only pay if you love it.

How long is the full story?+

Around 15 minutes of narrated audio. Long enough for bedtime, a rainy afternoon, or quiet time. Every story is unique and written from scratch.

Can I share it with family?+

One tap. That is all it takes. Hit share and send the story to grandparents, aunties, uncles, or anyone you like by email or WhatsApp. They get a link, they tap play, done. You can also download the audio and save it straight to your phone. No extra cost, no limits on who you share it with.

What if I don't like the full version?+

Just let me know and I will write you a brand new one. No hassle, no questions. If you would rather just get your money back, drop me an email within 24 hours and I will sort it out, provided the story has not been downloaded or shared yet.

I have more than one child. Can they all be in it?+

Absolutely. Add siblings, cousins, or anyone you like using the family and friends fields. They will each get a real role in the story. If you want a separate story where another child is the hero, you will get a discount code after your first purchase.

What ages does this work for?+

Ages 2 to 14. Stories automatically adapt to the child's age. Toddlers get simple language with sound effects and repetition. Older children get richer plots, real dialogue, and deeper themes.

Can I listen to it more than once?+

As many times as you like, forever. The story lives in your personal library on this site. You can also download the audio file and save it to your phone, tablet, or computer. No expiry, no limits.

How long does the full story take to generate?+

About 5 minutes from start to finish. You will see a progress screen while it is being written and narrated.
