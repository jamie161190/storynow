Common questions

Is it safe? What do you do with my child's details?+

We only use your child's details to create their story. Nothing is shared, sold, or stored beyond what's needed. The story is yours. We take child safety seriously.

Can I hear it before I pay?+

Yes. You get a free preview of the opening of your child's story before you spend anything. Only pay if you love it.

How long is the full story?+

Around 15 minutes of narrated audio. Long enough for bedtime, a car journey, or quiet time. Every story is unique and written from scratch.

Can I share it with family?+

Absolutely. Share it with grandparents, aunties, uncles, anyone, via WhatsApp or email. No extra cost, no limit. You can also download the audio to keep forever.

What if my child doesn't like it?+

We'll rewrite it for free. No questions asked. We want every child to love their story.

Is £19.99 for one story?+

Yes. One completely unique, 15 minute personalised audio story with your choice of narrator. No subscription, no hidden costs. You own it forever.
