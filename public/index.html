Common questions

Can I hear it before I pay?+

Yes. You get a free preview of the opening of your story before you spend anything. Only pay if you love it. Lots of parents make them as gifts too.

How long is the full story?+

Around 15 minutes of narrated audio. Long enough for bedtime, a rainy afternoon, or quiet time. Every story is unique and written from scratch.

Can I share it with family?+

We built sharing right in. Once your story is ready, tap share and send it by email or WhatsApp in seconds. Grandparents, aunties, uncles, whoever you like. You can also save the audio straight to your phone. No extra cost, no fuss.

What if I don't like the full version?+

Just let us know and we will generate you a brand new one. No hassle, no questions. We want you to love it as much as they will.

Can I gift this to someone?+

Yes. After you choose the story type, we ask if this is a gift. Tell us who it is from, add a message, and we will send them a beautifully designed email with the story inside. Perfect for birthdays, Christmas, or just because.

Is £19.99 for one story?+

Yes. One completely unique, 15 minute personalised audio story with your choice of narrator. No subscription, no hidden costs. You own it forever.
